# Medical and Clinical Use Disclaimer

## Intended Use

SlicerSEEGRecon is an open-source software extension for 3D Slicer developed to facilitate the reconstruction, visualization, and analysis of stereoelectroencephalography (SEEG) implantations. It is intended for research, educational, and clinical support purposes.

The software is designed to assist users by automating and simplifying several tasks commonly performed during postoperative SEEG reconstruction. It is **not** intended to replace expert clinical judgment.

## Not a Medical Device

SlicerSEEGRecon has not been certified, approved, or cleared as a medical device by any regulatory authority.

The software should not be considered a substitute for commercially certified medical software where such certification is required.

## Clinical Responsibility

Some components of SlicerSEEGRecon, including the automatic CT-to-MRI registration algorithm, have been validated on an internal dataset. However, no algorithm is infallible, and performance may vary depending on image quality, acquisition protocols, anatomical variability, and other factors.

Users are solely responsible for reviewing and verifying all generated results before using them for clinical interpretation, surgical planning, patient management, or any other medical purpose.

In particular, users should visually inspect image registrations, electrode reconstructions, anatomical assignments, and all derived outputs before relying on them.
